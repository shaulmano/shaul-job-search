HOW TO SEND EMAILS
==================

1. Open PowerShell (Start -> search "PowerShell")

2. Copy and paste this command:

& "$env:LOCALAPPDATA\Programs\Python\Python312\python.exe" "C:\Users\Shaul\Documents\job-search\send_emails.py"

3. For each email:
   y = Send
   n = Skip
   q = Stop

IMPORTANT: Send 10-15 emails per day maximum to avoid spam filters.

FILES IN THIS FOLDER
====================
outreach_list.xlsx  - The email list (Status column tracks what was sent)
companies.xlsx      - Add new companies here, then re-run build_outreach_list.py
send_emails.py      - The sending script
build_outreach_list.py - Finds recruiters and emails for new companies
find_companies.py   - Updates the companies list
